About Me

Hello I'm Afnan
I have been watching Korean dramas for more than 10 years. From 2011 till now, Kdrama (As referred to as Korean drama) was part of my daily life. At the end of each day, watching some episiods of Kdramas became a routine for me. And to be honest, I don't like "routine " word because it seems like something I "just do" usually, but in reality, it's something I really enjoy doing. It's part of my small precious world. I believe that even from watching you can learn so many new things! In the 10 years, from the dramas I watched, I learned so much valuable things! I will share with you here my favorite Kdrama. My favorite genres are Romantic Comedy drama, phycological, crime, and melodrama.
